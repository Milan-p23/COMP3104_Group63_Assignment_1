COMP 3104 - DevOps is a comprehensive course designed to introduce students to the principles and practices of DevOps in software development. The course focuses on the integration of development and operations to enhance collaboration, efficiency, and continuous delivery.
Students will explore key concepts such as Continuous Integration (CI) and Continuous Delivery (CD), learning how to automate the software development lifecycle using various tools and techniques. The course covers essential topics like version control systems (e.g., Git), automated testing, containerization (e.g., Docker), and orchestration tools (e.g., Kubernetes).
Through practical assignments and projects, students will gain hands-on experience in setting up CI/CD pipelines, managing infrastructure as code, and monitoring applications in real-time. The course emphasizes collaboration among team members and the importance of communication in a DevOps environment.
Additionally, students will learn how to utilize cloud services and environments to facilitate scalable and reliable software delivery. By the end of the course, participants will be equipped with the skills and knowledge to implement DevOps practices effectively, preparing them for careers in modern software development and operations. COMP 3104 aims to provide a solid foundation in DevOps, fostering an understanding of its role in the software industry today.
