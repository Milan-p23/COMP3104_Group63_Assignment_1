George Brown College Information:


George Brown College is located in Toronto and offers a variety of programs, including technology, health sciences, and business.

•⁠  ⁠Nursing

George Brown has modern facilities such as the Centre for Health Sciences and the School of Design and Computer Science.

healthcare settings. The School of Design offers students a chance to explore various design disciplines.
The School of Design is located in the heart of downtown Toronto, providing students with access to a vibrant
